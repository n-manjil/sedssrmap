Two members from SEDS SRM AP secure victory at the National-level Quiz Competition hosted at IIT Kharagpur.

December 29

The Space Club of SRM AP, SEDS, is delighted to announce the outstanding success of Team Cosmic Comets - comprising Shail Preet, 2nd year BSc Physics, and Sri Aarush Aray, 2nd year BTech CSE. They have secured the prestigious first prize at the Space Quiz 12 Finals organized by 'spAts,' a student body affiliated with the Kalpana Chawla Space Technology Cell, held at IIT Kharagpur from October 6 to 8, 2023. Heartfelt congratulations to our brilliant space champions on this remarkable achievement!
